Biomes Project

Work together as a class to create a display of 7 different biomes. Your display should have the following:

  • 3D Elements
  • At least 4 different elements per biome.
  • An index card for each biome with:
      Name of biome
      Koppen Climate classification of that biome
      5 specific real world locations of that biome
      5 Characteristics of that biome
      Name of all students who worked on that biome
